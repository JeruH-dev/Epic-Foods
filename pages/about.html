Our promise

Launch with trust before adding heavy systems

For a first launch, Epic Foods needs clear product pages, working cart and wishlist flows, visible delivery information, customer contact channels, and a dependable order form. Payments, inventory dashboards, and delivery automation can be connected after the storefront is stable.

Fresh sourcing

Products are selected from trusted farmers, markets, and suppliers.

Clean packing

Orders are grouped, checked, and prepared for quick delivery.

Human support

Customers can follow up on availability, delivery, and returns.

Delivery

Same-day local delivery can be offered inside Uyo for confirmed morning orders.

Returns

Damaged or incorrect fresh items should be reported on delivery with a photo.

Payments

The order page is ready for bank transfer, pay-on-delivery, or a payment gateway.
